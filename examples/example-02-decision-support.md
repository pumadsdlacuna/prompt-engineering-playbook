# Example 02 – Decision Support Prompt

## EN
This prompt is designed to support structured decision-making in a business context.
The goal is not creativity, but clarity, trade-offs, and risk awareness.

## ES
Este prompt está diseñado para apoyar la toma de decisiones estructurada en un contexto de negocio.
El objetivo no es creatividad, sino claridad, trade-offs y conciencia de riesgos.

---

## Use Case / Caso de Uso

**EN**  
A business leader needs to decide between multiple strategic options using limited information.

**ES**  
Un líder de negocio necesita decidir entre varias opciones estratégicas usando información limitada.

---

## Prompt Structure

### Role
"You are a senior business analyst with experience in strategy, finance, and risk management."

### Context
"The company operates in a highly regulated industry and is evaluating three possible initiatives."

### Task
"Analyze the options provided and recommend the best course of action."

### Constraints
- Do not assume missing data
- Clearly state uncertainties
- Use structured bullet points
- Avoid generic advice

### Output Format
1. Summary of options
2. Comparative analysis (pros / cons)
3. Risks and mitigation
4. Final recommendation

---

## Why this works / Por qué funciona

**EN**
- Forces explicit reasoning
- Reduces hallucination
- Aligns output with executive expectations

**ES**
- Obliga a razonamiento explícito
- Reduce alucinaciones
- Alinea la salida con expectativas ejecutivas
